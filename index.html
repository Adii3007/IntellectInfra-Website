ABOUT US

Delivering Excellence in HVAC, VRF & Air Conditioning Solutions

Intellect Infra Solutions is a trusted name in the HVAC industry, with over 20 years of experience delivering high-efficiency centralized VRF air conditioning systems for residential, commercial, and corporate spaces. We provide tailored HVAC design, installation, ducting, and maintenance services to ensure reliable performance, energy efficiency, and long-term comfort. Partnering with leading brands like LG and Mitsubishi Electric, we deliver scalable and smart solutions backed by transparent service and timely execution.

OUR SERVICES

HVAC Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) & Emergency AC Repair Services

Intellect Infra Solutions offers end-to-end HVAC Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMCs) and emergency AC repair services tailored for commercial and industrial facilities. Our preventive maintenance plans are crafted to enhance system efficiency, reduce energy consumption, and ensure continuous operation. We provide full-time on-site technician support when needed, along with discounted service rates and a readily available inventory of essential HVAC and electrical spare parts for faster response and minimal downtime.
